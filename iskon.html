ISKON Temple

  • Iskcon is known all over for being some of the most beautiful, active, spiritual and religious centres in the country.
  • One such temple, Iskcon Hyderabad can be found in the Nampally region.
  • The main deity in Iskcon Hyderabad temple is Lord Krishna.
  • One can also see idols of Sri Gaura-Nitai, Sri Jagannatha Baladeva-Subhadra and Sri Radha Madana-Mohana.
