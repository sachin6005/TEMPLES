Birla Mandir

  • The Birla Mandir in Hyderabad is dedicated to
    the Lord Venkateshwara of Tirumala.
  • It is in fact modelled after and is the replica of the idol of Balaji of Tirupati
  • It Created in 1976 by Swami Ranganathananda from Ramakrishna Ashram
    and constructed by the Birla Foundation
